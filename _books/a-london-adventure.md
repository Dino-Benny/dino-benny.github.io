Dino and Benny ride up top through the busy streets, hear Big Ben go bong, and watch Tower Bridge open wide for a tall boat. Ravens hop by the old stone tower, guards in tall furry hats stand very still, and the London Eye lifts two best friends high above the whole city. "Brilliant!" says Benny.

There are fish and chips to crunch, a dinosaur to meet at the museum (imagine that!), and tea and biscuits when the rain comes. On go the wellies, up go the brollies, and a rainbow shines over the river to end a perfect day. Cheerio, London!

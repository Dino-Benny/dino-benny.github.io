Dino and Benny arrive by bullet train and learn their first word right away: konnichiwa, with a bow. They watch a whole crossing full of people step off at once when the light turns green, and meet Hachiko, the statue of a good dog who waited for his friend every single day. A great red lantern hangs in the temple gate, little shops line the lane with toys and fans, and a pagoda rises with five roofs to count.

The day unfolds gently. Pink petals fall like snow over a picnic of rice balls, orange koi splash in the pond, and a kind lady folds paper into a crane, so Dino tries a hat and Benny tries a boat. Little plates of sushi ride past on a belt, taiko drums go boom, and up at the top of the tallest tower in the world, far-off Mount Fuji shows its snowy top. Then night falls, the noodles slurp, and the whole glowing city says sayonara.

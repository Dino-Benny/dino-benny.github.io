About the series

The Adventures of Dino and Benny began with a simple idea: young children deserve stories where the world is wide, warm, and worth exploring. No villains, no scares, no lessons learned the hard way. Just two best friends discovering real places side by side.

Dino is a curious green dinosaur with a blue backpack. Benny is a calm brown bear with a red scarf. Neither one leads and neither one follows; they figure things out together, as equals. In Paris they watch the Eiffel Tower sparkle. In New Orleans they dance to a brass band. In New Delhi they light little lamps for Diwali. In Tokyo they fold a paper crane. Every landmark, food, and word in the books is real, so each story plants a small, true seed about the world.

The books are written at an early-reader level: short sentences, playful sounds, and rhythms that make read-aloud time easy for grown-ups and fun for kids who are starting to read along. Each book stands alone, so families can begin anywhere.

The series is written by S. & H. Rajan and published by Dino and Benny, LLC. Many more adventures are on the way, from Rome and Rio de Janeiro to stories about eclipses, kite flying, and celebrations around the world. The friends have a lot of ground to cover, and they would love for your family to come along.
